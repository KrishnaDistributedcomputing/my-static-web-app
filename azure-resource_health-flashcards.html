Azure Service Health Flashcards

Test your knowledge about Azure Service Health with these flashcards. Click on a card to reveal the answer.

What is Azure Service Health?

A suite of experiences providing personalized guidance and support when Azure service issues affect you.

What are the components of Azure Service Health?

Azure Status, Service Health service, and Resource Health.

What does Azure Status provide?

A global view of the health of Azure services across all regions, including service outages.

What does Service Health provide?

A personalized view of the health of Azure services and regions you use, including outages and maintenance.

What does Resource Health provide?

Information about the health of individual cloud resources, helping you diagnose and get support for issues.

How can you get notified about Azure service issues?

Set up Service Health alerts to notify you via your preferred communication channels.

What are the types of health events tracked by Service Health?

Service issues, planned maintenance, health advisories, and security advisories.

What is the purpose of the Service Health portal?

To provide a customizable dashboard tracking the health of your Azure services in the regions you use.

What does the Resource Health status 'Available' mean?

There are no events detected that affect the health of the resource.

What does the Resource Health status 'Unavailable' mean?

The service detected an ongoing platform or non-platform event affecting the resource's health.

What is the 'Unknown' status in Resource Health?

Resource Health hasn't received information about the resource for more than 10 minutes.

What is the 'Degraded' status in Resource Health?

The resource detected a loss in performance, although it's still available for use.

How can you access Resource Health for a resource?

Sign in to the Azure portal, browse to your resource, and select Resource health from the menu.

What is the new Health Alerts experience in Service Health?

An updated interface for better usability, allowing users to search, sort, and view alert details and history.

What feature has been deprecated in the new Service Health experience?

Users can no longer pin a personalized map to the dashboard.

What is the importance of the Azure Status page?

It provides a global view of the health of Azure services and regions, showing service availability and incidents.

How can you create a Service Health alert using the Azure portal?

Select Service Health, go to Health alerts, and add a new service health alert.

What information can Resource Health provide during an issue?

Details about the event, recovery process, and actions you can take, including contacting Microsoft Support.

What types of notifications can Service Health alerts provide?

Notifications for service issues, planned maintenance, health advisories, and security advisories.

What is the benefit of setting up Service Health alerts?

Ensures you are proactively notified about health issues affecting your Azure resources.
